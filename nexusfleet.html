Nexus Fleet

News & Guides hub for Star Trek Fleet Command

Nexus Fleet is a side project I am working on. It's a dedicated editorial hub covering the game Star Trek Fleet Command. It provides community guides, updates, and community-driven insights for players who want to stay ahead in their favorite universes.

What I did

  • Designed and developed the website in React with a custom CMS.
  • Created content workflows for publishing news, guides, and events.
  • Integrated SEO best practices and analytics for growth tracking.
